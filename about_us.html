Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.)




  • Can I build a shelf?

  • How much does it cost?

  • What material should I use?

  • What tools do I need?




Can I build a shelf?

Use our site to find the tools that you need to build your own shelf. With enough time and at least some basic tools anyone can make their creation a reality. If you need some help designing your shelf or shelves, see our photo gallery for inspiration

How much does it cost?

The cost for your shelf will largely depend on the material and complexity of your design. Common materials and basic designs will typically be less expensive.

What Material Should I use?

Your project can be built from almost any material imaginable. Your budget and ability to find material will likely define your material needs.

What Tools Do I Need?

Generally, basic hand tools will be required for any installation. Construction of shelves will require several tools. See below to get an idea of what is needed:

Tools For Installation

  1. Tape Measurer
  2. Level
  3. Hammer and Nails
  4. Stud Finder
  5. Power Drill
  6. Screwdrivers

Tools that May Be Required For Building Shelves

  1. Table Saw
  2. Sander (power or manual)
  3. Chisel
  4. File or rasp
  5. Router
